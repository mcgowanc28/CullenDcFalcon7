Who Is Greater

Getting down to the last parts of the debate.

Gretzky

Gretzky had a amazing career, he dominated all of his seasons. He finished his career with 1487 games played, 894 goals, 1963 assists, and a whopping final 2857 points and a +520 in plus minus.

Ovechkin

Ovi also had a outstanding career, he proved how good he was early winning the rookie of the year. He finished with 1491 career games, 897 goals, 726 assists, and 1623 career points.

To end it off, Wayne Gretszky is the better player in this debate. Even though OVI is a great player, wayne has the better stats, accolades, and is more of a skill player. If most people were asked, they would most defintly want gretszky on a team other than ovi. Ovi is a great player who can do it all, but gretszky is one of a kind with multiple 200 point seasons which most players now get around 80-140 in 82 games.

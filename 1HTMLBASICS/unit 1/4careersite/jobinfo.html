FireFighter

Description

As a firefighter, you have a lot of responsibiltes to take care of. Some of those include, Respond to emergencies, Drive firetrucks and other emergency vehicles, Put out fires using water hoses, fire extinguishers, and water pumps, Find and rescue occupants of burning buildings or other emergency situations, and treat sick or injured people.

46,000-82,000 yearly
